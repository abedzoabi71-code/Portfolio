Who We Are

Fourteen years of care.
Built around you.

Vitalcore was founded by Dr. Noa Friedman in 2011 with one principle: that private medical care should feel personal, not transactional. Our team of four senior specialists brings together orthopaedic therapy, sports rehabilitation, and integrative wellness — all under one roof on Rothschild Boulevard. We don't rush appointments. We don't apply generic protocols. Every patient leaves with a plan that was written for them.

What We Offer

Our treatments

Each treatment is carried out by a named specialist — not rotated between staff. You see the same clinician every visit, so nothing gets lost between sessions.

01
Initial Consultation

A 60-minute one-on-one assessment covering your history, movement patterns, and goals. You leave with a clear diagnosis and a written treatment plan — same day.

02
Deep Tissue Therapy

Clinically directed manual therapy targeting chronic tension, restricted fascia, and muscular imbalances. Effective for desk workers, athletes, and post-surgical patients alike.

03
Rehabilitation & Recovery

Structured programmes for injury recovery, post-operative care, and return to sport. Progress is tracked at every session and your plan adjusts as you improve.

04
Wellness Packages

Prepaid blocks of 5 or 10 sessions at a reduced rate, designed for patients who want consistent, ongoing care rather than ad-hoc appointments.
